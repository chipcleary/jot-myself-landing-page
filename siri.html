Using Siri

Talking is the easiest way to jot.

And that means Siri.

Jot Myself supports two approaches.

1. Use Siri's Built-In Phrases

Jot Myself supports the phrases Siri already knows for sending messages.

On your watch or iPhone, say:

  • Hey Siri, use Jot Myself to send a message, or
  • Hey Siri, send a message using Jot Myself, or
  • Hey Siri, message myself using Jot Myself.

2. Create a Custom Phrase

Want an even simpler way to jot?

On your iPhone, create a custom shortcut phrase (e.g., "Jot myself!").

You can create a shortcut when you first set up Jot Myself.

Or you can do it later.

Afterwards, just say Hey Siri, <your shortcut phrase>! (e.g., Hey Siri, jot myself!).

Bonus: Use Your Shortcut on Your Watch

If you create a shortcut on your phone, you can add it to your watch.

Open the Shortcuts app that Apple provides (e.g., on your phone, say Hey Siri, open Shortcuts!).

Find your shortcut.

Long press on it (press it and hold down until the menu appears).

Then tap Details

Tap the Show on Apple Watch toggle. It will turn green.

Then tap Done

To give it a try, say Hey Siri, <your custom phrase>! to your watch.
