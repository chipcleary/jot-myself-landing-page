Why Jot Myself?

You're good at managing to-dos …

You've adopted one approach or another. And it works great when your thoughts make their way into it.

… but you still miss “loose ends.”

Sometimes thoughts crop up when it's too awkward to interrupt what you're doing, pull up an app, and jot down a note.

Start capturing them!

Use Jot Myself to capture loose ends as they arise without distracting yourself.

How you might use Jot Myself?

What kind of loose ends might you capture? Consider how Anne goes through her Monday:

To Dos

Headed into the office, Anne remembers it’s her turn to lead the team meeting next week.

She jots herself a reminder to pick up funny hats for the ice breaker.

Ideas

Grabbing coffee, Anne browses her favorite subreddit, r/homesteading. She wonders whether that oddball duck pond might be just the ticket for her own backyard.

She jots herself to think it over.

Connections

In a project meeting, the team seems stuck on a marketing issue. Anne realizes that her friend Sam has dealt with similar issues.

She jots herself to ask for his advice.

Issues

While brainstorming a design for another project, Anne remembers that a project manager still hasn’t been assigned to it.

She jots herself to follow up with her manager.

Opportunities

Biking home, Anne notices a new restaurant has just opened near where she lives.

She jots herself to to check it out.

Anne sends herself a half-dozen jots during the day to corral the loose ends that would otherwise slip by her.
