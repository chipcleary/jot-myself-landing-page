Why Jot Myself?

You're good at managing to-dos …

You've adopted one approach or another. And it works great when your thoughts make their way into it.

… but you still miss “loose ends.”

Sometimes thoughts crop up when it's too awkward to interrupt what you're doing, pull up an app, and jot down a note.

Start capturing them!

Use Jot Myself to capture loose ends as they arise without distracting yourself.

How It Works

It takes just a few seconds to capture a jot.

  • iPhone

1
Say “Hey Siri, jot myself” and dictate your jot.

2
Your transcribed jot goes to your email inbox.

How you might use Jot Myself?

What kind of loose ends might you capture?

To Dos

Headed into the office, Sam remembers it’s his turn to lead the team meeting next week.

He jots himself to pick up squoosh balls for the ice breaker.

Ideas

Grabbing coffee, Anne browses her favorite subreddit. That self-filling duck pond might be just right for her own backyard!

She jots herself to think it over.

Connections

In Arvind's project meeting, the team gets stuck. Arvind's friend Amelia has dealt with similar issues.

He jots herself to ask for her advice.

Issues

While refining a design, Leticia realizes that the original timeline now looks dicey.

She jots herself to flag the risk to the team.

Opportunities

Biking home, Ludovic notices a new restaurant has just opened nearby.

He jots herself to to check it out.

Over the course of a day, users may send a half-dozen or more jots to corral loose ends that would otherwise slip by.
